print club

a monthly subscription service for postcard-sized, bug-themed prints

available at:

https://www.patreon.com/BaileyFoltz

about :

What is it?

Print Club is a monthly subscription service where, for $7 every month, i will mail you one postcard-sized print.

What do the prints look like?

Each month features its own print starring a unique bug - the print (and the featured bug) for each month will be a surprise, BUT the theme for all of the prints is bugs and insects.

How do I join?

Visit me at patreon.com/baileyfoltz and join the 'Print Club Subscription' under the Membership tab! Make sure to read the description for any additional info that may be relevant to you and feel free to reach out with any questions.

bugs :

January - orchid mantis

February - io moth

March - great green bush-cricket
