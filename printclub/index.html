print club

a monthly subscription service for postcard-sized, bug-themed prints

[ 2025 ]

about

Print Club was a monthly subscription service where, for $7 every month, I mailed subscribers one postcard-sized print and one bug sticker.

Each month featured its own print starring a unique bug - the print (and the featured bug) for each month was be a surprise, BUT the theme for all of the prints was bugs and insects.

the bugs

January - orchid mantis

February - io moth

March - great green bush-cricket

June - jumping spider

April - ladybug

May - cicada

July - flower chafer beetle

September - dragonfly

August - stickbug

October - roly poly

November - carpenter ant

December - spicebush swallowtail
