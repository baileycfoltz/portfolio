Bailey Foltz

Bailey Foltz is a multidisciplinary graphic designer, brand designer, and illustrator based in New York. With a degree in Interactive Media Arts from NYU and a passion for analog art, her work sits at the intersection of art and technology, allowing her to bring a multifaceted and thoughtful approach to digital design. She creates designs that are whimsical, tactile, and intentional.

When she’s not designing, you’ll likely find her hanging out with cats, volunteering with heart of dinner, studying tagalog, or curating posts for her instagram story.
