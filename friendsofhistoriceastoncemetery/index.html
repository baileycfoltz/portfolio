friends of historic easton cemetery

A logo design and brand identity creation for Friends of Historic Easton Cemetery, a nonprofit supporting the Easton Cemetery

Recognizing the unique historic, environmental, and educational assets, the Friends of Historic Easton Cemetery, a 501(c) (3), works to preserve, protect and promote the Easton Cemetery (established 1849).

We are a Nonprofit who supports Easton Cemetery with events, programs and outreach. We want to shift the focus of cemeteries so they are approachable, open to everyone, and not exclusive to folks who have loved ones buried inside. Easton Cemetery is a greenspace, and a park for all.
