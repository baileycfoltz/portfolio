green traill 5k

A logo design and brand identity creation for the 2025 Green Traill 5k, a fundraising race in a cemetery

Deliverables: logo design, visual system guidelines, printed assets, digital assets

about :

"Join us for a 5K Run, a 2 Mile Walk, and a Kids Fun Run through scenic roads among spring foliage and historic monuments. Enjoy post race beverages and snacks with the community on Sunday, May 4, 2025 from 9:00 am - 11:00 am at Easton Cemetery.

Funds raised from this event will be used to protect, preserve and promote this community resource and greenspace."

logo :

The primary logo is based on the iconic traill green statue in the easton cemetery, the namesake of the race.

This illustrated version of traill green running provides a friendly mascot to represent the race and brings to life the rich and captivating history of the cemetery.

typography :

A custom lettering is used for the title as it appears in the logo and additional marketing materials.

Superclarendon - A serif font inspired by vintage concert posters, this font combines the sophistication of serif with more friendly rounded curves and width variation to remain playful.

Raleway - A sans-serif font inspired vy vintage concert posters, this simple, narrow font provides clear contrast to the design of superclarendon.

color palette:

The primary color - Green has been chosen as the primary color for several reasons - to imitate the weathered green of the Traill Green statue, to represent the 'green' in Traill Green, and to evoke the greenness of the nature in the cemetery.

The secondary color - A bright orange has been chosen to evoke energy, friendliness, and to offset the softer green.

The tertiary color - An off-white has been chosen to soften backgrounds and be less demanding on the eyes than a stark white.
