three streams brewery

A simple logo redesign and brand identity presentation for Three Streams Brewery, an independent brewing organization

Deliverables: logo design, visual system guidelines, printed assets

about :

"Three Streams is an independent brewing organization founded on the simple concept of experimentation with a desire to share exceptional brews and a willingness to drink the failures alone. We brew a range of beers, ciders, and meads within the profiles we personally like to drink."

While Three Streams is not a company and does not sell any products, they still create original brews to share amongst family, friends, and each other, and they often enter their brews in various local fairs and competitions.
