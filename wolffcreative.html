wolff creative

A logo design for Wolff Creative LLC, a creative consulting company

Deliverables: logo design, printed assets

about :

Amy Wolff is a Creative Consultant. With a background in editorial photography as a photographer and photo director, and production and marketing experience spanning beyond the photo world, Wolff can make your project come to life.

Wolff operates under Wolff Creative LLC, providing Creative Production, Event Production, Art Direction, and Photo Editing services.

logo :

The primary logo showcases a dynamically running wolf, a direct nod to Wolff’s last name. The image of the wolf symbolizes intelligence, freedom, strength, and community while the logo embodies an energetic yet approachable presence, steering clear of aggressive or ferocious connotations.

To ensure versatility, secondary and tertiary logo variations are designed for square and circular layouts. In these versions, the wolf is depicted in a stationary, curled-up position - alert yet composed.

Custom lettering for ‘Amy Wolff’ and ‘Wolff Creative’ complements the logo, ensuring a cohesive and distinctive brand identity across all applications.
