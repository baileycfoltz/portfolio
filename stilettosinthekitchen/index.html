stilettos in the kitchen

A logo design and instagram for Stilettos in the Kitchen, a certified health coach and home cook

Stilettos in the Kitchen is a wellness platform by Sarika Bansal, a certified health coach and home cook. Rooted in confidence and nourishment, Stilettos in the Kitchen shows that you can love food, live well, and still keep things simple. Her mission is to simplify the world of healthy eating and make nourishment both delicious and joyful – crafting “healthy recipes so simple, you can make them in your stilettos”. Through this platform, she shares nutrition education, balanced meal ideas, and wellness insights rooted in her experience as a health coach and food scientist.
