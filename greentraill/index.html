green traill 5k

A logo design and brand identity creation for the 2025 Green Traill 5k, a fundraising race in a cemetery

Photos courtesy of Adam Hoff @adamhoff

Join us for a 5K Run, a 2 Mile Walk, and a Kids Fun Run through scenic roads among spring foliage and historic monuments. Enjoy post race beverages and snacks with the community on Sunday, May 4, 2025 from 9:00 am - 11:00 am at Easton Cemetery.

Funds raised from this event will be used to protect, preserve and promote this community resource and greenspace.

Photos courtesy of Adam Hoff @adamhoff
